Home Watch - For Peace of Mind

The process begins with meeting you at your home to learn about your individual requirements to understand how we can best tailor our services to your needs. We aim to provide the services necessary to ensure that your home will be properly cared for while you're away. We conduct regularly scheduled visits following your departure, which include a inspection and walk-through of your property, both inside and out.

Outdoor Inspection

From garden maintenance, lighting, waste collection, irrigation system, window/shade condition, rodent inspections, etc.

Internal Inspection

Regular interior inspection on things like security systems, lighting, water/faucet inspection, checking water heaters, smoke detectors, etc.

Beyond The Home

Whether you need a ride from the airport, a car battery replaced, your home prepared for arrival, or someone to be present at the home for a service, we'll be there!

Outdoor upkeep includes but is not limited to: junk mail accumulation, garden maintenance, lawn maintenance, patio furniture upkeep external waste collection, sand and debris, intrusion, window/screen/shutter condition, and irrigation system management.
Indoor upkeep includes but is not limited to: secruity system operation, interior lighting inspection, insect/rodent invasion, general home condition check, checking all appliance operation, inspection of gas and water settings, smoke detector checks, and periodic running of water faucets as needed.
Beyond the home services for all clients needing services performed outside the boundaries of their home. This includes airport pick up as needed, car battery replacement, meeting repair or upkeep service professionals at your home, and more! If a service is not listed, just ask!
